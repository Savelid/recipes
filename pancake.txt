Ingredients:
 - 50 g butter
 - 6 dl milk
 - 3 eggs
 - 3 dl flower
 - 1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions:
  1. Mix flower and salt with half the milk until even.
  2. Add butter eggs and the rest of the milk
  3. Mix
  4. Cover the botton of a hot fryingpan and fry until the pancakes got a bit of a color
  5. Turn around, fry other side.
